Coffee

  • About Coffee:

A beverage made from roasted coffee beans, grown in over 70 countries. It is primarily cultivated in North and South America, and is also used in East Asia, the Indian subcontinent, and Africa.

Green coffee beans are said to be the second most traded commodity in the world after crude oil. Because they contain caffeine, coffee can have a stimulating effect on humans. Today, coffee is one of the most popular beverages worldwide.

The earliest reliable evidence of either coffee drinking or knowledge of the coffee tree appears in the mid-15th century.Coffee originated in Sufi monasteries in Yemen, in the southern Arabian Peninsula.It spread from Ethiopia to Yemen, Egypt, and the Arabian Peninsula By the 15th century, it had reached Armenia, Persia, Turkey, and North Africa. Coffee spread from the Islamic world to Italy, then to the rest of Europe, Indonesia, and the Americas.

Coffee fruits, which contain coffee beans, are produced by several species of evergreen shrubs belonging to the coffee tree. The two most common species are Coffea canephora (also known as Coffea robusta) and Coffea arabica; the less common species are Liberica, Excelsa, Stenophylla, Mauritiana, and Racemosa Coffee beans are primarily grown in Latin America, Southeast Asia, and Africa. Once ripe, the beans are harvested, processed, and dried. They are then roasted, undergoing several physical and chemical changes. The roasting process varies depending on the desired flavor. Finally, the beans are ground and brewed to make coffee, which can be prepared and served in a variety of ways.
